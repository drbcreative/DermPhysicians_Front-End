HOW IT WORKS

The non-invasive HIFEM® procedure induces powerful muscle contractions not achievable voluntarily. Due to these contractions, muscle tissue is forced to adapt to extreme conditions. The body responds with a deep remodeling of its inner structure, resulting in muscle building and body sculpting!
